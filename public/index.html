Best Movies Ever According to Me

A list of my all-time favorite films!

Spirited Away (2001)

A beautiful bildungsroman animated film by Hayao Miyazaki about a young girl navigating a world of spirits.

Arrival (2016)

A thought-provoking science fiction film directed by Denis Villeneuve, exploring themes of language, time, and humanity through the lens of first contact with an alien species.

Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

A visually stunning and humorous superhero film directed by Taika Waititi, featuring Thor's journey of self-discovery and redemption while facing off against the powerful Hela.

Honorable Mention

Pluto (2023)

A Netflix limited series beginning as a murder mystery in a world where sentient robots live alongside humans. As the plot unfolds into an action-driven story, it probes deep philosophical themes: war, ethics, and what it means to be human; ultimately challenging viewers to reconsider legal and moral responsibilities toward sentient life. It is both incredible and devasting and is one of those pieces of media you only watch once but stays with you for the rest of your life.
